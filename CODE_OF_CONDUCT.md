# Code of Conduct

AI+SU is maintained as a practical tool for SketchUp users and developers. Contributions should keep the project useful, safe, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific when discussing bugs, design choices, or tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent, but stay clear about risks and regressions.
- Keep issue reports actionable.
- Redact API keys, private drawings, customer data, and personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private data or credentials.
- Sharing exploit details in public issues when a private report is appropriate.
- Repeated off-topic comments that make maintenance harder.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users.
